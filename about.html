About

Green Daughters is the creation of two friends and one daughter as a space to display jewellery and other little gifts. For now, it a space to showcase our jewellery and other crafts, with the hope to graduate into the ability to sell our products on-line. When our world goes back to somewhat "normal", it is the hope to set up a table at local craft fairs in the Ottawa, Ontario area.

Carla

I have been practicing the art of making chainmail jewellery since 2015, when I took my first jewellery making class and loved it. My progress is ever-evolving as I find more time to practice different weaves and incorporate beads and gemstones into my pieces. I also enjoy making wire wrap jewellery and elasticized gemstone bracelets, but find I gravitate more towards the chainmail. If you have any questions about what you see on the site, please don't hesitate to contact us!
